Do you want to start your appeal now?

Your appeal to the tax tribunal will cost £20

What are fees for?

  • your fees are paid to HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) towards the cost of an independent judgement
  • you may have to pay further fees depending on your case and whether it needs a hearing
  • all tax tribunal fees will be refunded by HMRC (or other government agency) if your appeal is successful
  • if you’re on a low income or receiving certain benefits, you should first apply for help with fees

What you'll need

  • your name and address (and your representative’s if you have one)
  • copy of the review decision letter from HMRC to upload (e.g. a scan or PDF)
  • details of your dispute, why you’re appealing and the result you want
  • credit or debit card to pay your appeal fee

What happens next

You can start your appeal by entering your details. You'll be able to save your appeal information and return later to complete everything and pay your fee.
