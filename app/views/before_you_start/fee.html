Your fees

Before your appeal can start, you will need to pay £20 for the tribunal to process your case.

In cases like yours, there is typically no need for a hearing in person and there will be no further fees to pay.

You may be able to get help paying your fee if you’re getting certain benefits or on a low income.

Refunds

The tribunal judge is independent of government and will listen to both sides of the argument before making a decision. They also have the power to decide whether you should be reimbursed for any costs including your tax tribunal fees.

If the tribunal rejects your appeal or the judge decides not to award you costs, you will not be able to claim back any tax tribunal fees.
