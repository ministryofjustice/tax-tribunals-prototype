Paying for your appeal

You will have to pay the initial fee, or enter a help with fees reference number, to submit your appeal successfully.

To pay by cheque or postal order, you will have to print out the details you enter online and send your appeal by post.

You will also have to pay a further fee towards continued work on your appeal. The further fee is payable once your case is in progress.
