When you submit an appeal for the amount of VAT or any 'indirect' tax you owe, you must do one of the following before the tax tribunal can consider your appeal.

  • pay the amount owed upfront
  • ask HMRC to defer payment, this is known as a hardship application

You do not have to apply for hardship if your appeal is about a penalty or surcharge.
