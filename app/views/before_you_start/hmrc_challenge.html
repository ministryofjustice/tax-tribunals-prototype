Help with challenging a tax decision

The original notice letter will say how you can query or challenge a tax decision.

You can still appeal to the tax tribunal after you have challenged the decision.

If you accepted or asked for a review, you should wait until the review ends or allow 45 days for it to be carried out before appealing. Your appeal risks being rejected if a review is ongoing.

Find out more about how to challenge a tax decision with HMRC.
