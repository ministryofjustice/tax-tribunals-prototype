Apply to close an ongoing enquiry

Force HMRC to issue a closure notice for an ongoing enquiry. You will need to pay an initial fee of £50 to submit an appeal unless you're eligible for help with fees.

You will also have to pay a further fee if your case needs further documents, correspondence and a hearing in person with a judge.

See how much your appeal or application might cost
Table explaining the fees for different types of appeals or applications
Type of appeal or application Initial fee Further fee
  • Apply to close an enquiry
    (e.g. issue a closure notice)
 £50 £200

The judge is independent and has the power to decide whether your fees are refunded by the unsuccessful party. The tribunal itself does not have the power to enforce or award costs.

Check if you're able to get help paying your fees if you’re on a low income or receiving certain benefits.
