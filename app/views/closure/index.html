Apply to close an enquiry

You will have to pay an initial fee of £50 to submit your application unless you are on a low income or certain benefits.

You will also have to pay a further fee of £200 towards the cost of a hearing and work on your case through to its completion.

The judge has the power to decide whether your fees are refunded by the unsuccessful party.

What you will need

  • details of the enquiry you want closed including the HMRC reference number and name of the tax officer involved
  • realistic time for the enquiry to close so the judge can order HMRC to issue a closure notice by a certain date
  • signed approval (PDF, 58Kb) if you want someone to act on your behalf and receive letters from the tax tribunal or attend a hearing
