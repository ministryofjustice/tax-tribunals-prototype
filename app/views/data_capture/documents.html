For your case to proceed without delay you need to make sure you upload the correct documents. We can accept scanned images or good quality pictures, for example via a smartphone.

You will need:

  • The original notice letter from HMRC including the explanation for any decision(s)
  • The review conclusion letter from HMRC (if your case was reviewed)
  • Any hardship decision by HMRC or letter to HMRC applying for hardship (if applicable)

You can also add the following to help your case:

  • A copy of any letter sent to HMRC querying the decision or asking for a review
  • Any evidence to support your case
  • Approval for your representative to act on your behalf
