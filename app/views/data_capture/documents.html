For your case to proceed without delay you need to make sure you upload the correct documents. We can accept scanned images or good quality pictures, for example via a smartphone.

Documents to upload:

  • A copy of the HMRC decision(s) received from HMRC
  • A copy of any statement of reasons for that decision(s)
  • A copy of any notice of conclusions from a review by HMRC
  • A copy of any letter sent to HMRC asking for a review
  • A copy of any hardship decision by HMRC or any letter to HMRC regarding hardship (if applicable)
