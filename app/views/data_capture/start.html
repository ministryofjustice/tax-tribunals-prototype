You will need to include grounds for your appeal and upload a scan or photo of the letters from HMRC.

What to get ready before you start

  • a scan or photo of the original notice letter or review conclusion letter from HMRC ready to upload
  • write down the reasons why you disagree with the decision(s) by HMRC so you can copy and paste the text or attach as a document
  • if you're completing this as a representative and you're not a legal professional, you'll need an email or scan of a letter from your client saying they can act on your behalf
  • a debit or credit card to pay the initial tribunal fee unless you're eligible for help with fees

Warning You can't save details as you go. Please get everything ready before you start.
