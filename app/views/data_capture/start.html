You will need to include grounds for your appeal and upload a scan or photo of the letters from HMRC.

What to get ready before you start

  • digital copies of the decision letter(s) - scan or take a photo of the original notice letter (or review conclusion letter if you had a review)
  • reasons why you're appealing - write out your grounds of appeal so they are ready to copy and paste or upload as a document
  • a way to pay your initial tribunal fee - you can pay by debit/credit online or check first if you can get help with fees if you're on a low income or certain benefits
  • approval to act for someone else - you will need authorisation from the taxpayer (PDF, 58KB) to receive correspondence or act as their representative
  • other supporting documents - upload digital copies of documents to support your case including your hardship application if applicable

Warning You can't save details as you go. Please get everything ready before you start.
