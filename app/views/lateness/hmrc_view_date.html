Do you think you're in time to appeal to the tax tribunal?

Help with tax tribunal deadlines

You have 30 calendar days to appeal to the tax tribunal. This time starts from the date shown on either the original notice letter or review conclusion letter if you had a review.

If you are waiting for a review

If you accepted or asked for a review, you must allow time for the review to finish. You can only appeal once you have received a review conclusion letter or 45 days passes without receiving one.
