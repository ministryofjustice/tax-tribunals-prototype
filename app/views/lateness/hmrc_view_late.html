Why are you late with your appeal?

You must provide reasonable grounds for appealing late. If the judge rejects your reasons, your appeal will not proceed and any fees will not be refunded.

Help with late appeals

You must provide reasonable grounds for submitting your appeal late. These are usually unforeseen circumstances or events beyond your control which meant you weren't able to meet the tax tribunal deadlines.
