2. Check you meet tribunal deadlines

You have 30 calendar days to submit an appeal. You can still appeal late if you have good reasons.

The deadline to appeal begins from the date shown on either the original notice letter or review conclusion letter.

If you accepted or asked for a review, you can only appeal once you have received a review conclusion letter or 45 days passes without receiving one.

What you will need

  • date of the decision letter - this will be shown on either the original notice letter or review conclusion letter
  • reasons if you want to appeal late - the judge will consider your reasons and decide whether to accept or reject your appeal
