Appeal against a tax decision

You have 30 calendar days from the date of the decision letter to submit an appeal.

You can still appeal late with good reasons. The judge will decide whether to accept your appeal after the deadline.

What you will need

  • a scan or photo of the original notice letter, or review conclusion letter, so it's ready to upload in a digital format
  • written reasons for your appeal to copy and paste or attach as a document
  • signed approval (PDF, 58Kb) if you want someone to act on your behalf and receive letters from the tax tribunal or attend a hearing

Warning You can't save details so get everything ready before you start
