Send your appeal by post

You will need to send everything by post before the 30 day appeal deadline ends.

  1. Print out your case details

    Use the following link to print your case details

    TaxPayer Name Case details (PDF, 124 KB)

  2. Write out a cheque for £20

    Please make your cheque payable to 'HM Courts & Tribunals Service'

  3. Get copies of your decision letters

    Enclose a copy of either the original notice letter or review conclusion letter. Please note the tax tribunal cannot return any documents.

  4. Send everything together by post

    You must put your printed case details, cheque and copies of decision letters in one envelope. The tax tribunal address is:

    First-tier Tribunal (Tax Chamber)
    PO Box 16972
    Birmingham
    B16 6TZ

Once the tax tribunal has received your appeal they will write to you with details of the next steps.
